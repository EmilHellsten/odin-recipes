Horneater white

Horneater white is a distilled alcohol consumed in the Horneater peaks of Roshar. Generally Rosharan people don't consume straight ethanol, but the Horneaters are known for their potent liqour.

Ingredients

  • Lemon slice
  • Ice cubes
  • A bottle of Horneater white strong enough to clean Dalinar's stormwagons.

Steps

  1. Grab a glass.
  2. Juice the lemon into the glass.
  3. Add ice cubes and the liqour.
  4. Enjoy!
