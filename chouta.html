Chouta

Chouta also originates from the Rosharan system. More specifically, its of Herdazian origin. Chouta coult perhaps most closely be compared to a stuffed tortilla. In universe, chouta is a flat bread filled with some mystery meat of unknown origin and vegetables covered in a gravy.

Ingredients

  • Mystery meat
  • Flatbread
  • Gravy
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat.
  2. Stuff the flatbread with said mystery meat and veggies.
  3. Add the gravy on top.
  4. Enjoy!
